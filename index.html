Our Introduction

Welcome to Biogen

Biogen is an Organic Agriculture Solution Company. The aim of the company is to establish natural agriculture practices to grow and source non chemical agriculture produce in Sri Lanka whilst empowering the farmer community in Sri Lanka.

Let's heal the earth - heal the farmer
Heal the farmer - let's heal the country
  • Safer working conditions.
  • Greater biodiversity
  • Reduced leaking
  • Reduced erosion and better water management

What We Offers

Our biofertilizer product line

Carbonic Fertilizer

Golden Treatment

Additional nutrients and growth stimulants for the plant in the growth and flowering stage.

Rhysofier

In preparing the soil, in the growth phase and the flowering phase, provides the plant with essentials and micro-nutrients.

Rhysosara

Strengthens the root network of the plant, stimulating the fresh root growth. Fulfils the phosphorus requirements of the plant.

Rhyzoden Insect Deterrent

A tried and tested remedy for insect damage to leaves, trunk and fruit of the plant.

Rhyzone Green

Provides nutrients and microorganisms to the plant in the growth phase.

Rhyzone White

Provides nutrients and microorganisms required in preparing soil.

Rhyzone Yellow

Provides nutrients and microorganisms to the plant in the flowering phase

Seed Rejuvenator

Safeguards the seed’s Sprouting, strengthening the young plant. Improves the plant’s immunity, minimising the effects of pesticides
