Our Support for the Community

Biogen Sustainable Development

Our intention is simple: to use our own resources from our land to create livelihoods and empower communities, while ensuring poison-free local produce for Sri Lankans from all walks of life.

Not only will this initiative stop the bleed of foreign currency for the import of poisonous chemical fertilizer, but strengthen Sri Lanka’s economy in two ways. It will save foreign currency, while encouraging local entrepreneurship, as it grows Gross Domestic Production. Not only will this be a medium to long-term solution for the foreign exchange crisis Sri Lanka is currently facing, but a sustainable and instant solution for the looming food crisis.

Biogen Community Empowerment

  • Popularize the manufacture of organic raw materials and compost among farmers, while providing the technical knowhow and financial strength to do so.
  • Initiate a program to purchase crops that use organic fertilizer and additives in order to protect farmers and their livelihoods.
  • Provide practical farming solutions through technical expertise, provided via Farmer Development Organizations.
  • Strengthen Farmer Collectives by using the profits dedicated to develop farming communities, creating regional farming communities that possess the expertise and experience to solve a plethora of practical problems.
