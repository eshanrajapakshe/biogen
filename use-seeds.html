Product Usage

Usage of Biogen Seeds

Instructions for use

Add enough rhizosphere soil host, topsoil and rhizon prepared medium to your bed or pot, and plant the seeds in it.

On planting stage:

  • Plant at a depth of 1-2cm from the surface of the soil.
  • Apply water in the morning and evening in the early days, then once a day is sufficient.
  • Be careful as insects (ants) may eat the seeds as the sugar content increases when the seeds are mixed with water in the soil.
  • After 7 days expose to sunlight.
  • Do not apply anything other than water for 14 days. (Since the seeds are protected by biological natural methods and avoid using with synthetic chemicals.
Ninety-quality, 100% natural hemp seeds help you get the most out of your crop.
