About Biogen

We provide Organic Agriculture Solutions

Biogen is an Organic Agriculture Solution Company. The aim of the company is to establish natural agriculture practices to grow and source non chemical agriculture produce in Sri Lanka whilst empowering the farmer community in Sri Lanka. As the first step of a long term vision of being an organic agriculture solution company, as the initial step Biogen has embarked on a journey to provide Biogenic fertilizer to the farmers and institutions.

Through the strategic partnerships of Biogen Organic (Pvt) Ltd, the company intends on brining the best organic Agri solutions such as Fertilizer, Seeds, Agri Solutions & Equipment to the farmer directly to empower the farmer communities. Furthermore Biogen Organic (Pvt) ltd has initiated pilot programmers to enable farmers and uplift the livelihoods of them by partnering up to ensure guaranteed by backs to secure their income.
